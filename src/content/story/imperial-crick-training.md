The coursework covered deterministic and probabilistic modeling, experimental and theoretical systems biology, and core synthetic biology methods. For my project, I built a lab automation-enabled co-culture screening platform and paired it with an R analysis pipeline to map cross-feeding interactions across an auxotrophic yeast knockout library.
